Hi there!

I'm a soon-to-be graduate of the University of Kansas and a designer from the Midwest. I’ve been in love with art and design since the day I picked up my first crayon. Constantly drawing, doodling and making things as I grew up, I always knew that I wanted to pursue a creative career. While I spend most of my days on the computer, I am passionate about finding ways to work with my hands. I’m a firm believer in the power of a strong concept with an unexpected execution.

I'm currently working with my friends at Wedge & Lever while I pursue further opportunities in the world of information architecture and experience design.
