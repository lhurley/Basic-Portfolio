I'm a soon-to-be graduate of the University of Kansas. I've been in love with art and design since the day
I picked up my first crayon and stayed up past my bedtime to mess around on apple paint. Constantly drawing, doodling and making things as I grew up, I always knew that I wanted to pursue a creative career. While I spend most of my days on the computer, I am passionate about finding ways to work with my hands. I'm a firm believer in the power of a strong concept with an unexpected execution.

I'm currently working with my friends at Wedge & Lever while I pursue further education to benefit my role there as an information architect and experience designer.
